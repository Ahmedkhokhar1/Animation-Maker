Pro tips

Pro tips for animation makers everywhere

Ready to take your animation making prowess to the next level? Follow these pro tips for video animation success.

Keep your animation style consistent

Biteable’s in-house team makes new animated video scenes daily. With so many good ones, it’s tempting to use as many as you can. However, your video animation will look far more professional if you stick with similar animation sets – you don’t want it to look like you’ve cobbled together a bunch of mismatched clips.

Luckily, our template library groups similar animation scenes together so you’ll spend less time picking and more time editing your masterpiece.

Shorter is (usually) better

It’s a tried and true rule that you want to keep your online content short and sweet – the same goes for video animation. Just as you want to pare your scenes down to the bare essentials to tighten your video, do the same with your brand message.

What are you trying to say? What is the simplest and shortest way of saying it?

Here’s a tip: don’t put more than one idea or bullet point in a single scene. That way, viewers can easily take in the on-screen text and the animation accompanying it.

Customize and brand your video

Whatever colors or music your brand uses, you can include them in your animated videos. Choose a soundtrack from our library or upload your own music. Take your video animation to the next level by adding a voice over.

When you’re ready to brand your video, we’ve got you covered.

Biteable’s intuitive video editor fetches your brand colors directly from your website and automatically adds them to your entire video. It’s that simple.
